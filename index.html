Bring Me the Horizon (BMTH) is a British rock band formed in 2004 in Sheffield. Known for blending metalcore, electronic, and pop, their sound has evolved over the years.

Key albums include **"Sempiternal"** and **"That's the Spirit,"** featuring themes of mental health and personal struggles. With a dedicated fanbase, BMTH is celebrated for their innovative approach to music.
